Favorite Movies

Kiss(2019)

Kiss has a perfect mix of romance, fun, and emotions for a romance comedy entertainer. Sreeleela's acting is delightful to watch, and her cute expressions and natural performance make the movie more enjoyable.

Bhagavanth Kesari(2023)

Bhagavanth Kesari has a good mix of emotions, fun, and an engaging story. Sreeleela's performance adds more heart to the movie, and the climax fight makes it a memorable experience.

By Two Love(2022)

By Two Love has everything I enjoy in a romance drama, with emotions, relationships, and a realistic story. Sreeleela's cute and natural acting as a mother makes the movie more special, and her down-to-earth performance feels genuine and heartfelt.
